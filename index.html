Hi, this is simple gravity toy i wrote to practice programming in JavaScript.

If you want to look at the code, clicking at my name at the top of the page or here will take you to my Github profile.

If you want to just play with gravity, here are some keyboard controls and corresponding commands in bluish colour, if you're kind of person who likes to use console (or something is broken with keyboards controls):

Arrows / Left Mouse Button + Mouse - Moving the camera around

Page Up - zooming in camera.zoom = [value]

Page Down - zooming out camera.zoom = [value]

Space - freezing/unfreezing the time time.toggle()

V - spawning 100 random small objects

B - spawning 10 random big objects

N - spawning static object hold

M - spawning 50 highspeed objects

randomSpawn( [how many?]* , [minimum size]* , [maximum size]* , [distance span]* , [velocity span]* )

"*" means that these parameters are default, and you don't have to specify them

spawing area is zoom dependent.

C - Deleting all objects from scene clear()

> - Speeding the time time.speed = [value]

< - Slowing the time time.speed = [value]

Increase in time speed will also increase calculations inaccuracy!

] - Increasing G by 1 G = [value]

[ - Decreasing G by 1 G = [value]

Alt - Show objects info

F5 - Reload page When something breaks down (like when you change tab for a while)

F11 - Full screen shortcut mode in most browsers

F2 - Help This window
